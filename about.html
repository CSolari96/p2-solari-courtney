About Florida FFA Alumni & Supporters

Chartered in 1972, the Florida FFA Alumni Association works to develop a partnership with the members of the Agricultural Education family in Florida - including the Florida FFA Association, Florida FFA Foundation, Florida Association of Agricultural Educators, and the University of Florida Agricultural Education and Communications Department - in order to provide resources to encourage and enhance the development of premier leadership, personal growth, and career success of FFA members.
